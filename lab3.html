The Lab

This Lab Focuses on Responsive Web Design, Using Bootstrap we will be creating a responsive website that can be viewed from Computers, Tablets & Mobile Devices


Please link to this page from your main website, and submit the URL to your main website & Commit Hash for the lab

The Requirements

  • a title and Sub Header (In jumbotron)
  • A Nav Bar to bring you back to your homepage and to show lab3 as your currentpage
  • at least one paragraph
  • at least one list (ordered or unordered) with at least three list items
  • at least one image (Rounded)- in the same grid as your list
  • one horizontal rule With date + Author Name

Some Tips!

  • read all the requirements first
  • save changes and check in browser after each change
  • make sure you name your file lab3.html and link to it from your index under your links section
  • You should make sure all your tags are balanced if they are supposed to be. There are only a few tags which do not have ending tags, like hr and br.

Submitting Your Lab

Include link to latest commit hash + your website URL, commit hash must be before Lab due date to be considered
